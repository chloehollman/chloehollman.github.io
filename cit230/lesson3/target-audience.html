Target Audience

The target audience for this are those who are looking for a nice beach to go to. Many people go to the beach for sunny weather, so they need a website to tell them which beaches have the best weather.

Personas

Name: Karen Smith, 39, stay-at-home mom

Name: Steven Spielburg, 24, financial broker

Scenarios

-A girl at BYU-Idaho just got engaged and is looking for honeymoon destinations. She wants to go to a beach that is warm and sunny.

-A Men's Beach Volleyball team is looking for a beach in California that could settle their needs. They don't want it to be too hot, but they also don't want it to be too cold to play.

-A mother is taking her children to the beach for the first time. She wants to settle for a place with warm enough weather so the kids will enjoy it more.

-An 18 year old boy is looking for a place to take his high school photos. He wants to take them on the beach, and needs good weather to do so.

-A few teenage girls want to go to the beach for the summer to get a tan. However, they want the sunniest and most warm beach in California.
